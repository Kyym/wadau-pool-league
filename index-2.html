HOW POINTS ARE CALCULATED
Just like in football — every individual frame (game) you win earns you 3 points. So Kim winning 15 frames today = 45 points. Henry and Simon both won 7 frames = 21 points each. When points are tied, the player with the better frame win% ranks higher.
Pts = Wins × 3
P — Frames Played today
W — Frames / Games Won
L — Frames / Games Lost
Win% — W ÷ P × 100
Pts — W × 3 (like football)
Tiebreaker → Win%
